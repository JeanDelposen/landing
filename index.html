Who are we?

ESSEC Esports is a thriving community within ESSEC Business School dedicated to competitive gaming and fostering a vibrant gaming culture among its students. Founded on the principles of teamwork, skill development, and sportsmanship, ESSEC Esports provides a platform for students to engage in various esports titles, ranging from popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games like League of Legends and Dota 2 to first-person shooters like Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and Valorant. Through regular tournaments, workshops, and social events, ESSEC Esports not only cultivates a competitive spirit but also promotes camaraderie and inclusivity within the gaming community at ESSEC. With a passionate and dedicated student body, ESSEC Esports continues to grow, contributing to the broader landscape of collegiate esports in France and beyond.
